## Compliance Verification

### Step 1: Identify Applicable Standards
- Review product requirements
- Identify applicable standards
- Document in compliance matrix

### Step 2: Verify Compliance
- Review design against standards
- Verify documentation compliance
- Test compliance (if required)
- Document verification

### Step 3: Document Compliance
- Complete compliance matrix
- Document verification methods
- Update compliance status

## Compliance Status

**Compliant:** Meets all requirements  
**Non-Compliant:** Does not meet requirements  
**Partial:** Meets some requirements, gaps identified
